Playing & Coaching Background

Sam Kwak is a Killeen area native and is very excited to be back to grow tennis in the area. He has competed/taught all over the world and he’s proud to be able to come back to his hometown to help the next generation of tennis players in Central Texas.

Sam played NCAA Division 2 College Tennis at Ferris State University and graduated with a bachelors in Professional Tennis Management. Sam has also competed on the ITF Professional Circuit and reached the 2nd round of the ITF $25k Futures and also got to compete against top 300 world ranked players. He currently competes in USTA Open and UTR Events. He has been ranked as high as #2 in Men’s Open Singles in Texas as well as attaining a Top 500 finish in the nation.

Tennis Programs

High Performance Program is designed for players who are making the commitment to play on their high school team and/or usta tournaments players looking to take their game to the next level. This program will implement challenging drills along with competitive live ball points to improve players tactics as well as mental toughness. Fitness and mental sessions will also be incorporated in the program.

Private Coaching

Private lesson are recommended for players looking to speed up their progress with 1 on 1 training. Private lessons supplemented with group programming is the fastest way for a player to progress.
